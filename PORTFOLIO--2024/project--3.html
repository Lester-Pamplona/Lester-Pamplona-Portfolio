InstaPay Back-Office Portal

Role

lead UI Designer

Year

2024

Introduction

The InstaPay Portal Back-Office is designed to seamlessly manage all participating banks that utilize InstaPay for electronic fund transfers (EFT) to PNB Money Transfer. This powerful platform offers real-time monitoring, management, and reporting of interbank transactions, ensuring smooth and secure fund transfers between financial institutions.

Process

The process involves creating wireframes for a low-fidelity prototype to study system usability across all age groups, gathering feedback for refinement, then developing a high-fidelity prototype, conducting advanced user testing, and iterating before final design handoff, followed by continuous improvement post-launch.
