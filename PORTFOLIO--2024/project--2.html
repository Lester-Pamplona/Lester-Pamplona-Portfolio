PNB Remittance App

Role

UI/UX Design

Year

2024

Introduction

This is the PNB Remittance App, an exclusive service for PNB clients designed to facilitate fast and secure money transfers. With a user-friendly interface, clients can easily monitor exchange rates, add beneficiaries, and track recent transactions. The app provides essential account settings, security options, and service-related information such as transaction fees and payout partners

Process

In the Process stage, the workflow begins with creating a user flow to map out the user journey. Next, a low-fidelity design is developed to visualize basic structure and layout. Once the user flow and low-fidelity design are finalized, the focus shifts to creating high-fidelity designs and building an interactive prototype for more detailed testing and refinement.
